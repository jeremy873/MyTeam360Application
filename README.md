## Overview

MyTeam360 is a privacy-first AI platform that lets users create, customize, and manage AI Spaces — persistent workspaces powered by any AI provider. Build agents with plain English, import existing assistants, and keep every byte of data under your control.

## Key Capabilities

- **14 AI Providers** — Anthropic, OpenAI, Google, Mistral, DeepSeek, Cohere, Together, Perplexity, Fireworks, Groq, xAI, Azure, AWS Bedrock, Ollama
- **71 Models** — Full model selection across all providers
- **Smart Cost Routing** — Auto-selects optimal model per message
- **Writing Voice Learning** — AI learns your writing style over time
- **Roundtable Discussions** — Multi-agent + multi-human collaborative sessions
- **Compliance Watchdog** — 18-rule real-time content scanning with 3-tier escalation
- **Sales Coach** — RFP analysis, interview prep, objection handling, pitch scoring
- **Digital Marketing** — Country-aware campaign planning across 10 markets
- **Learning DNA** — Adaptive education that detects student struggles
- **Content Guardrails** — Curriculum enforcement with parental consent
- **Global Context** — 10 country profiles with local laws, customs, and languages
