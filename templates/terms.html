Analogy: MyTeam360 is to AI what a web browser is to websites. We provide the window through which you access AI services. We don't control what those services produce any more than Chrome controls what websites display.

We are not an AI provider. We do not generate AI responses. We provide the interface, agent configurations, and infrastructure that connects you to the AI providers of your choice.
