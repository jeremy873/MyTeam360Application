  • Security: Detect abuse, prevent unauthorized access, enforce safety policies

We do NOT use your data for: advertising, profiling, selling to third parties, training AI models, or any purpose other than operating the platform.

4. AI Provider Data Flow

When you send a message, it is routed to the AI provider you selected (Anthropic, OpenAI, Google, etc.) using YOUR API key. The message content flows directly from our server to the provider's API and back. We do not store a copy of the provider's response beyond what is saved in your conversation history.

Important: Each AI provider has its own privacy policy and data practices. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of the providers you use. MyTeam360 acts as an intermediary — we route your data but do not control how providers process it.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you have some or all of the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of all data we hold about you (/api/data-export/full)
  • Deletion: Request permanent deletion of all your data (/api/security/gdpr/erase)
  • Portability: Export all your data in JSON format (/api/data-export/full)
  • Correction: Update your profile information at any time
  • Objection: Object to specific processing activities
  • Restriction: Request restricted processing of your data
  • Withdraw consent: Withdraw consent for optional processing at any time

To exercise these rights, email legal@myteam360.ai or use the in-platform tools. We respond within 30 days (45 days for CCPA requests).

8. Security

  • All data encrypted at rest with AES-256-GCM
  • Optional zero-knowledge encryption (we can't read your data)
  • Passwords hashed with bcrypt (12 rounds)
  • MFA/TOTP available with enforcement options
  • Content Security Policy, cookie hardening, HSTS
  • DLP scanning prevents accidental secret exposure
  • Log sanitization strips secrets before storage
  • Regular automated backups with encryption

See our Security Overview for complete details.
