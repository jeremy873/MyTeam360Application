How Learning DNA Works

After just 5 interactions, the platform knows if you learn best through examples, analogies, step-by-step breakdowns, or formal proofs.

It tracks which subjects you're strong in and which ones challenge you. When it detects frustration — "I can't understand this" — it switches to Intervention Mode: different approach, smaller steps, building on what you already know.

The DNA never goes negative. If you're having a bad day, it doesn't learn your frustration. It learns your topics, your structure preferences, and your vocabulary — but always responds with encouragement and growth.
