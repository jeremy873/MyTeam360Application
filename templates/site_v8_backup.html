FAQ

Common questions, straight answers.

Do I need technical skills to use this?
No. You talk to the platform and it configures itself. The voice concierge walks you through setup in a conversation — no forms, no settings pages, no documentation to read.
What does "Bring Your Own Key" mean?
You get your own API key from an AI provider (like Anthropic, OpenAI, or Google) and plug it in. You pay them directly for AI usage — typically $5-20/month. We never charge you for AI usage, and we never have access to your conversations.
Can I use it without any AI key?
Yes. CRM, invoicing, tasks, social scheduling, goals, expenses, booking links, and more all work without an AI key. The AI features (voice capture, briefings, roundtables, content generation) need a key. 77% of the platform works without one.
How is this different from HubSpot or Salesforce?
Those are databases you type into. MyTeam360 is an intelligence layer you talk to. It connects your calendar, email, weather, flights, and traffic into your CRM and briefings — things no traditional CRM does. And it costs a fraction of the price.
Is my data safe?
API keys are AES-256-GCM encrypted at rest. We never train models on your data. We never share it with anyone. Full audit trail on every action. SOC 2 ready architecture. You can export everything at any time.
What happens if I want to leave?
Export all your data anytime. No lock-in contracts. No exit fees. No "retention specialists." Cancel and your data is yours.
